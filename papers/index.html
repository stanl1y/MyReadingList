力覺 × 接觸豐富操作Force-Aware Contact-Rich Manipulation

這串的共同敵人:力訊號稀疏又吵——大多數時間接觸力≈0,天真地把力丟進 policy 只會讓它更黏視覺、甚至更糟。上位的立場文章(Karcini)主張機器人缺的是把力/具身/獎勵「接地」的機制;底下兩條線各自出招。SJTU · Cewu Lu 線走「控制與架構」:手持力捕捉+混合力位控制(ForceMimic)→ 用接觸預測 gate 掉噪音(FoAR)→ 用 Interaction Frame 拆出力控/位控子空間(Force Policy)。CMU · Pathak 線走「訓練與感測」:視覺模糊 curriculum 逼 policy 先學力(FACTR)→ 讓廉價手臂免感測器也能估力(FACTR 2)。The shared enemy of this bunch: force is sparse and noisy—contact force is ≈0 most of the time, so naively feeding force to a policy makes it lean on vision even more, or worse. The umbrella position paper (Karcini) argues robots lack mechanisms to ground force / embodiment / reward; two lines answer it. The SJTU · Cewu Lu line evolves control & architecture: handheld force capture + hybrid force-position control (ForceMimic) → gating noise via contact prediction (FoAR) → splitting force/motion subspaces with an Interaction Frame (Force Policy). The CMU · Pathak line evolves training & sensing: a vision-blur curriculum that forces the policy to learn force first (FACTR) → sensorless force estimation for commodity arms (FACTR 2).

Robots Need More Than VLAs & World Models

Karcini, Mehrban, Nguyen, Schwager, Ajoundani, Cadena, Peters, Hutter, Bou-Ammar

立場文章:通才機器人不只是 policy-scaling 問題;真正缺的是把海量非結構化行為資料「接地(ground)」成機器人可用監督的四種 interface——資料引擎、跨具身 retargeting、物理世界模型、獎勵接地。A position paper: generalist robotics is not just a policy-scaling problem; the missing layer is a set of interfaces that ground the world abundant unstructured behaviour into robot-usable supervision—a data engine, cross-embodiment retargeting, physics world models, and reward grounding.

ForceMimic: Force-Centric Imitation Learning with Force-Motion Capture System for Contact-Rich Manipulation

Wenhai Liu, Junbo Wang, Yiming Wang, Weiming Wang, Cewu Lu

用一個 $50、免機器人的手持式力-位置擷取器 ForceCapture 收集自然的「力覺」示範資料,再以 HybridIL(擴散策略同時預測位姿與力,並用正交的混合力-位置控制原語去追蹤),在削櫛瓜這種接觸密集任務上比純視覺模仿學習相對提升 54.5% 成功率。A $50 robot-free handheld force-motion capture rig (ForceCapture) collects natural force-centric demos; HybridIL then predicts both pose and wrench with a diffusion policy and tracks them via an orthogonal hybrid force-position controller, raising success on contact-rich zucchini peeling by 54.5% relative over pure-vision imitation learning.

FoAR: Force-Aware Reactive Policy for Contact-Rich Robotic Manipulation

Zihao He, Hongjie Fang, Jingjing Chen, Hao-Shu Fang, Cewu Lu

在 RISE 視覺策略上加入高頻力/扭矩感測,用一個「未來接觸預測器」動態調整力覺特徵的融合權重,並以此驅動 reactive control;僅靠簡單位置控制就在擦拭、削皮、切菜等接觸密集任務上大幅超越所有 baseline。On top of the RISE vision policy, FoAR adds high-frequency force/torque sensing and a future-contact predictor that dynamically gates the force feature during fusion, then drives a reactive controller; with only simple position control it far outperforms all baselines on wiping, peeling and chopping.

Force Policy: Learning Hybrid Force-Position Control Policy under Interaction Frame for Contact-Rich Manipulation

Hongjie Fang, Shirun Tang, Mingyu Mei, Haoxiang Qin, Zihao He, Jingjing Chen, Ying Feng, Chenxi Wang, Wanxi Liu, Zaixing He, Cewu Lu, Shiquan Wang

仿照人類「視覺管全局、力覺管局部」的分工,提出物理奠基的 Interaction Frame 與 global-local 雙頻 vision-force 策略,用高頻力回饋做 hybrid force-position 控制,在多個接觸密集任務上顯著超越強力基線。Inspired by the human split of labor—vision guides the global plan, force governs local contact—it introduces a physically grounded Interaction Frame and a global-local dual-frequency vision-force policy that does hybrid force-position control, beating strong baselines across contact-rich tasks.

FACTR: Force-Attending Curriculum Training for Contact-Rich Policy Learning

Jason Jingzhou Liu, Yulong Li, Kenneth Shaw, Tony Tao, Ruslan Salakhutdinov, Deepak Pathak

用「逐步解模糊視覺」的課程訓練逼機器人策略先學會用力覺,搭配一套低成本雙向力回饋遙操作系統,讓接觸密集任務對未見物體的泛化提升約 40%。A curriculum that progressively de-blurs the visual input forces a robot policy to first learn from force, paired with a low-cost bilateral force-feedback teleoperation rig, boosting generalization to unseen objects in contact-rich tasks by ~40%.

FACTR 2: Learning External Force Sensing for Commodity Robot Arms Improves Policy Learning

Steven Oh, Jason Jingzhou Liu, Tony Tao, Philip Han, Kenneth Shaw, Satoshi Funabashi, Ruslan Salakhutdinov, Deepak Pathak

用一個只吃 10 分鐘無接觸資料、訓練 1 分鐘的 LSTM(NEXT)在無力感測器的廉價機械臂上估測外部關節力矩,再用估到的力把示範切成接觸相位並上採樣(FIRST),讓行為克隆在五個接觸密集任務上平均任務進度提升 >17%。NEXT is an LSTM trained on 10 min of contact-free data (1 min of training) that estimates external joint torque on sensor-less low-cost arms; FIRST then uses that force to segment demos into contact phases and up-sample them, lifting behavior-cloning task progress by >17% across five contact-rich tasks.

VGGT: Visual Geometry Grounded Transformer

Jianyuan Wang, Minghao Chen, Nikita Karaev, Andrea Vedaldi, Christian Rupprecht, David Novotný

單一前饋 transformer,吃 1~數百張影像,<1 秒一次輸出相機參數、深度圖、point map、3D 點軌跡,且常勝過需昂貴幾何優化後處理的方法。A single feed-forward transformer that turns 1 to hundreds of images into cameras, depth maps, point maps and 3D tracks in under a second—often beating methods that need costly geometric post-processing.
