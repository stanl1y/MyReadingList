SECTION 01

Overview & The Shared Tension

TL;DR

這 5 篇是「力覺 × 接觸豐富操作」同一條研究線。共同敵人只有一個:力訊號稀疏又吵——大多數時間接觸力≈0,天真地把力丟進 policy 只會更糟。兩組人馬下不同的注:SJTU · Cewu Lu 線賭「控制與架構」,CMU · Pathak 線賭「訓練與感測」。上位的 Karcini 立場文章則說:機器人缺的正是把力/具身/獎勵「接地」的機制。

These 5 papers form one research line on force-aware contact-rich manipulation. They share a single enemy: force is sparse and noisy—contact force is ≈0 most of the time, so naively feeding force to a policy only makes things worse. Two groups place different bets: the SJTU · Cewu Lu line bets on control & architecture, the CMU · Pathak line on training & sensing. The umbrella Karcini position paper says: what robots lack is exactly the mechanism to ground force/embodiment/reward.

共同的敵人:力很稀疏、很吵The common enemy: force is sparse & noisy

整串的起點是同一個觀察:接觸力大多時候接近 0,只有在關鍵瞬間才有意義。因此「把力當成 policy 的另一個輸入通道」幾乎都失敗。有趣的是四篇各自獨立撞到同一面牆:

The whole line starts from one observation: contact force is near-zero most of the time and only matters at key moments. So "just add force as another policy input channel" almost always fails. Strikingly, four papers independently hit the same wall:

So each paper is, in essence, a different answer to "force is sparse & noisy."

SECTION 03

Two Lineages, Two Bets

SJTU · Cewu Lu 線

賭注:改「控制與架構」——把力用對地方Bet: fix "control & architecture"—use force in the right place
ForceMimic→FoAR→Force Policy

共同信念:不要把力硬塞進 policy,而要「結構化」力怎麼被使用。演進是一條「越來越有原則」的力位分解之路:預測力+混合控制(ForceMimic)→ 用學習到的接觸機率 gate 掉噪音(FoAR)→ 用 Interaction Frame 從物理剛度拆出力控/位控子空間(Force Policy)。三篇都長在 RISE / Diffusion Policy 視覺骨幹上。

Shared belief: don't cram force into the policy—"structure" how force is used. The evolution is an increasingly principled force-position decomposition: predict force + hybrid control (ForceMimic) → gate noise via a learned contact probability (FoAR) → split force/position subspaces from physical stiffness with an Interaction Frame (Force Policy). All three sit on the RISE / Diffusion Policy visual backbone.

CMU · Pathak 線

賭注:改「訓練與感測」——讓 policy 願意學力、讓力變便宜Bet: fix "training & sensing"—make the policy learn force, make force cheap
FACTR→FACTR 2

共同信念:力可以照舊當輸入,真正要解的是「policy 不肯學力」和「沒有力感測器」。FACTR 用視覺模糊 curriculum,在訓練早期把視覺弄糊、逼梯度流向力編碼器(學習動態層面的介入);FACTR 2 更進一步,讓廉價手臂免感測器也能自監督估出外力,把「有力訊號」的門檻壓到最低。走的是「資料 / 訓練 / 感測」而非「控制器」。

Shared belief: force can stay an input; the real problems are "the policy won't learn force" and "there's no force sensor". FACTR uses a vision-blur curriculum to blur vision early and push gradients toward the force encoder (an intervention at the learning-dynamics level); FACTR 2 goes further, letting commodity arms estimate external force sensorlessly, driving the "having a force signal" barrier to near zero. It works on "data / training / sensing," not the controller.

為什麼會分成兩條路?Why two paths?

兩組的起點不同就注定了路線:SJTU 從接觸豐富的家事任務(削皮、擦、刮)出發,這些任務「力該往哪個方向施」很明確,所以自然走向混合力位控制的顯式結構。CMU 從低成本遙操作 + 通用模仿學習(GELLO/ALOHA 傳統)出發,手上是粗糙的關節力矩而非乾淨 F/T,所以問題被框成「怎麼讓大 policy 別忽略這個吵訊號」+「怎麼免費拿到它」。[導讀補充] 這是我的歸納,非論文明述。

Their starting points predestined the paths: SJTU starts from contact-rich household tasks (peeling, wiping, scraping) where "which direction force should go" is clear, so it naturally moves toward the explicit structure of hybrid force-position control. CMU starts from low-cost teleop + generalist imitation learning (the GELLO/ALOHA lineage), holding coarse joint torque rather than a clean F/T signal, so the problem is framed as "how to stop a big policy from ignoring this noisy signal" + "how to get it for free." [guide note] my inference, not stated in the papers.

SECTION 04

Convergence & Tension

大家都在解的隱藏子問題:「我現在有接觸嗎?」The hidden sub-problem everyone solves: "am I in contact now?"

因為力只在接觸時有意義,「偵測接觸階段」成了整串反覆出現的必要零件——只是每篇用不同方式做:

Because force only matters during contact, "detecting the contact phase" becomes a recurring necessary component—each paper does it differently:

論文Paper怎麼知道「在接觸」How it knows "in contact"
ForceMimic硬門檻(6N)切換控制原語hard threshold (6N) switches control primitive
FoAR學習未來接觸機率 \(\phi\in[0,1]\)learned future-contact probability \(\phi\in[0,1]\)
FACTR 2\(\lVert\hat\tau_{ext}\rVert_1\) + 遲滯門檻切 free/接觸前/接觸\(\lVert\hat\tau_{ext}\rVert_1\) + hysteresis into free/pre-/contact
Force Policy用環境剛度(Interaction Frame)隱式界定受限方向uses environmental stiffness (Interaction Frame) to implicitly define constrained directions

從硬門檻→學習機率→物理幾何,可以看到「接觸偵測」本身也在被逐步做得更聰明。

From hard threshold → learned probability → physical geometry, contact detection itself is being made progressively smarter.

最有意思的正面對撞:ForceMimic vs FACTRThe most interesting head-on clash: ForceMimic vs FACTR

兩篇得到同一個發現——「把力當原始輸入,效果最差」——卻開出完全相反的處方:

Both reach the same finding—"force as raw input is worst"—yet prescribe opposite fixes:

ForceMimicFACTR
診斷Diagnosis力當輸入 → 沒學好force as input → not learned well
處方Fix改讓力當輸出 + 混合控制make force an output + hybrid control力仍當輸入,改訓練流程逼它學keep force as input, change training to force learning

這正是「控制派 vs 訓練派」哲學差異的縮影。

This is a microcosm of the "control school vs training school" divide.

取得力的趨勢Trend in getting force 越來越便宜:F/T 感測器(FoAR / Force Policy)→ 手持捕捉裝置(ForceMimic)→ 粗糙關節力矩(FACTR)→ 免感測器學習估計(FACTR 2)。降低「擁有力訊號」的門檻,是這串的暗線。 Ever cheaper: F/T sensor (FoAR / Force Policy) → handheld capture rig (ForceMimic) → coarse joint torque (FACTR) → sensorless learned estimation (FACTR 2). Lowering the barrier to "having a force signal" is a hidden throughline.
共用地基Shared substrate SJTU 三篇都建在 RISE / RISE-2(3D 點雲視覺)+ Diffusion Policy 上;CMU 兩篇建在 ACT / flow-matching 遙操作模仿學習上。骨幹選擇也分兩派。 The three SJTU papers build on RISE / RISE-2 (3D point-cloud vision) + Diffusion Policy; the two CMU papers build on ACT / flow-matching teleop imitation. Even backbone choices split along the two lines.
SECTION 05

Open Problems Across the Line

整條線共同的坑Pitfalls shared by the whole line

  • 評測都很窄:清一色 3–5 個真機任務、每任務 10–20 次、常無誤差棒。整個子領域的統計效力都偏弱,跨物體/跨任務泛化多半未測。
  • Evaluations are all narrow: uniformly 3–5 real-robot tasks, 10–20 trials each, often no error bars. The whole subfield is statistically weak; cross-object/task generalization mostly untested.
  • 沒有對上經典力控:幾乎都只跟自己的 vision-only / force-concat 變體比,很少正面對決 impedance / compliance / hybrid 傳統控制——「學習」相對「經典控制」到底贏在哪,不清楚。
  • No head-to-head vs classical force control: nearly all compare only to their own vision-only / force-concat variants, rarely against impedance / compliance / hybrid classical control—so where "learning" actually beats "classical control" is unclear.
  • 功勞歸屬糾纏:硬體(更好的力示範)vs 方法(curriculum / gating / IF)各貢獻多少,常沒被拆乾淨(FACTR 自承)。
  • Entangled credit: how much comes from hardware (better force demos) vs the method (curriculum / gating / IF) is often not disentangled (FACTR admits this).
  • 依賴特定條件:Force Policy 依賴閉源 Gemini 3 Pro 分類 + 高階硬體;NEXT 需逐台手臂重訓;多篇有一堆手調門檻。
  • Condition-dependent: Force Policy depends on closed-source Gemini 3 Pro + high-end hardware; NEXT needs per-arm retraining; several rely on many hand-tuned thresholds.

給讀書會 / 這條線的犀利問題Sharp questions for a reading group

  1. 公平對決:把 SJTU 的 Interaction-Frame 混合控制,和 CMU 的 curriculum+sensorless,放到同一組任務與硬體上比,誰贏?兩派能不能合體(便宜力估計 + 顯式力位分解)?
  2. A fair duel: put SJTU's Interaction-Frame hybrid control and CMU's curriculum+sensorless on the same tasks and hardware—who wins? Can the two combine (cheap force estimation + explicit force-position decomposition)?
  3. 接觸偵測的上限:硬門檻 / 學習 \(\phi\) / 剛度幾何,哪種在雜亂真實接觸下最穩?錯誤的接觸判斷會怎麼連鎖污染下游?
  4. Ceiling of contact detection: hard threshold / learned \(\phi\) / stiffness geometry—which is most robust under messy real contact? How do wrong contact calls cascade downstream?
  5. vs 經典控制:對 peg-insertion 這種老問題,這些學習法真的贏過調好的 impedance 控制嗎?贏在泛化還是贏在精度?
  6. vs classical control: on an old problem like peg insertion, do these learning methods actually beat a well-tuned impedance controller? Win on generalization or on precision?
  7. 免感測器的邊界:NEXT 的自監督力估計,在磨損、負載變化、跨機型時會漂移多少?能不能免重訓遷移?
  8. Limits of sensorless: how much does NEXT's self-supervised force estimate drift under wear, payload change, cross-model? Can it transfer without retraining?
⚠ 別過度外推Don't over-extrapolate
這串在接觸豐富、準靜態、家事尺度的任務上很有說服力;但別把它讀成「力覺操作已解決」——動態、高速、大變形、長程任務基本都沒被觸及。 This line is convincing on contact-rich, quasi-static, household-scale tasks; but don't read it as "force manipulation is solved"—dynamic, high-speed, large-deformation, long-horizon tasks are largely untouched.
回扣 KarciniBack to Karcini Karcini 說機器人需要「接地」機制;這 5 篇正是把其中「力」這一格做深。但其他格(具身 retargeting、世界模型、獎勵接地)在這串裡幾乎沒被碰——這串只是那張大圖的一角。 Karcini says robots need "grounding" mechanisms; these 5 papers deepen the "force" cell of that map. The other cells (embodiment retargeting, world models, reward grounding) are barely touched here—this line is one corner of that larger picture.
SECTION 06

Takeaways

怎麼用這條線How to use this line

  • 要做接觸豐富操作:先問自己站哪派——手上有乾淨 F/T 且任務力向明確 → 學 SJTU 的顯式混合力位 / Interaction Frame;只有廉價手臂、想走通用模仿學習 → 學 CMU 的 sensorless 力 + curriculum / 接觸上採樣。
  • Building contact-rich manipulation: first pick a camp—clean F/T and clear task force directions → adopt SJTU's explicit hybrid force-position / Interaction Frame; only a cheap arm and a generalist-IL goal → adopt CMU's sensorless force + curriculum / contact up-sampling.
  • 可直接借的零件:接觸機率 gating(FoAR)、力當輸出+混合控制(ForceMimic)、免感測器力估計(NEXT)、接觸段上採樣(FIRST)——這些都是模組化、可拆下來用的技巧。
  • Reusable parts: contact-probability gating (FoAR), force-as-output + hybrid control (ForceMimic), sensorless force estimation (NEXT), contact-segment up-sampling (FIRST)—all modular tricks you can lift out.
  • 最值得做的下一步:一個把兩派合體的 baseline(便宜力估計 → Interaction-Frame 混合控制),並第一次認真對上經典 impedance 控制。
  • Highest-value next step: a baseline combining both camps (cheap force estimation → Interaction-Frame hybrid control), and the first serious comparison against classical impedance control.
一句話結論Bottom line

同一個問題(力稀疏又吵),兩種世界觀:SJTU 相信「把力用對地方」靠更好的控制結構,CMU 相信「讓 policy 學會力」靠更好的訓練與感測。兩條線都對了一半——真正的通用接觸操作,大概需要它們合起來。

One problem (force is sparse & noisy), two worldviews: SJTU believes "use force in the right place" via better control structure; CMU believes "make the policy learn force" via better training & sensing. Each line is half right—general contact-rich manipulation will likely need both.
